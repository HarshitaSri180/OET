Frequently Asked Questions

The Occupational English Test (OET) is an English language test specifically for healthcare professionals to assess their communication skills in medical settings.

The OET consists of four components: Listening, Reading, Writing, and Speaking, each focusing on relevant healthcare scenarios.

You can prepare by enrolling in OET preparation courses, practicing with sample tests, and using various study materials available online.

The OET is conducted at various test centers worldwide. Check the official OET website for locations and upcoming test dates.
